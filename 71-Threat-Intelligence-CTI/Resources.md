## Key Terms Glossary
- **IOC**: Indicator of Compromise
- **TTP**: Tactics, Techniques, Procedures
- **Pyramid of Pain**: model illustrating the relative impact of blocking different IOC types
- **STIX**: Structured Threat Information eXpression
- **TAXII**: protocol for STIX exchange
