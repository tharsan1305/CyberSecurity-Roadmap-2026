## Key Terms Glossary
- **SIEM**: Security Information and Event Management
- **Correlation Rule**: logic connecting related events across sources/time to detect patterns
- **Alert**: output of a triggered correlation rule, routed to analysts or automation
- **SOAR**: Security Orchestration, Automation and Response (Topic 53, often paired with SIEM)
- **Log Aggregation**: centralizing logs from many sources into one searchable platform
