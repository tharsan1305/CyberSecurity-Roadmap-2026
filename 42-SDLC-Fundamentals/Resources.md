## Key Terms Glossary
- **SDLC**: Software Development Life Cycle
- **Sprint**: fixed time period (commonly 2 weeks) of focused work in Agile
- **Backlog**: prioritized list of work not yet scheduled into a sprint
- **Shift-Left Security**: addressing security earlier in the SDLC rather than only at the end (full coverage in Topic 51)
- **Standup**: brief daily team sync on progress and blockers
