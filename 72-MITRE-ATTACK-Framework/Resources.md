## Key Terms Glossary
- **Tactic**: the adversary's strategic goal (e.g., Initial Access, Persistence)
- **Technique**: a specific method achieving a tactic
- **Sub-technique**: a specific variation of a technique
- **Adversary Emulation**: simulating known threat actor TTPs to test defenses
